Spark is the place to share ideas, vote on what matters, and explore what others are building.

Post your idea in seconds. Browse the feed by trending or newest. Vote up the ideas you love. Dive into AI-generated specs and implementation plans for the most popular posts.

Features:
- Clean, fast feed with Hot and New sorting
- Category filters: Technology, Design, Business, Science, and more  
- Upvote and downvote with live scores
- AI enrichment: every popular post gets a generated spec and plan
- Idea Bases: generate a cluster of ideas on any topic with AI
- Comment threads on every post
- Sign in with Face ID or Touch ID
- Full offline support via PWA
- Native companion apps for iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch
